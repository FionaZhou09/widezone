# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Wide Zone Food serves restaurant operators, market buyers, distributors, and foodservice teams who need dependable Asian food sourcing in English or Chinese. Visitors are usually checking product availability, supplier credibility, contact details, or preparing a request for quote.

## Product Purpose

The site presents Wide Zone Food as a reliable wholesale food partner and lets buyers browse a bilingual catalog, filter products, build an RFQ basket, and submit purchasing details to the sales team.

Success means buyers can quickly understand the company, find relevant products, keep their preferred language across pages, and reach sales without friction.

## Brand Personality

Reliable, regional, professional, and supply-focused. The experience should feel established and business-ready without becoming generic corporate SaaS.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS landing-page gloss, decorative AI effects that do not support the buying journey, low-contrast luxury styling, placeholder imagery, and visual treatments that make the catalog feel less practical for wholesale buyers.

## Design Principles

- Lead with supplier credibility: make location, operational capability, and sales contact paths easy to find.
- Keep bilingual use calm: language choice should persist and never surprise the visitor.
- Support buying intent: catalog browsing, RFQ selection, and contact actions should stay direct.
- Use polish in service of trust: visual refinement should make the business feel capable, not ornamental.
- Preserve scanability: buyers should be able to compare categories, products, and next steps quickly.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA for contrast, focus states, keyboard navigation, and form labeling. Respect reduced-motion preferences. Maintain readable Chinese and English text at mobile and desktop breakpoints.
